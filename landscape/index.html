Manna-Landscape

Fast fair landscaping quotes built on rules, not guesswork.

`Manna-Landscape` is a private estimating and quoting tool being built for a real landscaping business. The goal is simple: faster quotes, cleaner records, and pricing math the owner can actually review.

Back to manna-core.dev
Current Phase

Early foundation with a working deterministic estimator and local quote desk.

Audience

Private single-user business workflow, not a public SaaS product.

Approach

Structured quote data, explicit business rules, and human approval before sending.

What It Solves

A cleaner way to quote real jobs

Core Outcome

Turn field conversations and measured work areas into saved quote drafts with clear totals, editable line items, and deterministic business math.

Current Service Coverage
  • Mulch installation
  • Pine straw installation
  • Bush removal
  • Bush relocation

Principles

What the product should stay true to

Rules First

Pricing logic should live in explicit code and business rules, not in prompts.

Owner Review

Nothing should be sent automatically. The owner stays in control of the quote.

History Matters

Saved quotes and completed jobs should improve future judgment instead of being lost.
