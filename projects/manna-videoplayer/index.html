A cleaner little window for watching videos.

Manna VideoPlayer opens YouTube links in a stripped-down local app-style window whenever the platform allows it.

When embedding is blocked, the fallback uses the normal YouTube watch page with cosmetic focus cleanup. It does not download, restream, bypass login, or bypass embed restrictions.

Current modes

Minimal embedded player, paste-and-open, focused watch fallback, and an experimental crop-fill fallback.

True north

Just the video, playback controls, captions, settings, volume, seek bar, and fullscreen.

Focus

Removes surrounding page noise when possible.

Honesty

Blocked embeds are treated as real platform restrictions.

Personal Utility

Built for Grayson's daily viewing flow, not as a video platform.

No Downloader

Does not copy or intercept streams.

No Bypass

Does not bypass YouTube access controls.

Cosmetic

The cleanup layer is UI focus, not content extraction.

Keep Small

The app should stay a focused player utility.

Manna VideoPlayer is the quiet utility side of Manna: one annoying workflow, one cleaner local surface.
